About WarpReader

WarpReader is a web-based speed reading app that uses RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) and AI comprehension coaching to help people read 2–3× faster without losing understanding. Users can upload any PDF, ePub, URL, or text — or browse a library of 61,000 free public domain books. WarpReader includes a 28-day training course, real-time WPM tracking, and AI-generated comprehension quizzes after every session. It's free to start with no download required.
