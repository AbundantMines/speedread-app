Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

SpeedRead is a product of Abundance LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SpeedRead (the "Service") at swiftread.app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google.

Usage Data: We collect reading session data including words per minute (WPM), session duration, number of documents read, and reading preferences to provide and improve the Service.

Payment Information: Payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full card details. Stripe provides us with a transaction ID, last four digits of your card, and billing email for record-keeping.

Device & Browser Information: We automatically collect browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address for analytics, security, and troubleshooting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve SpeedRead's features and performance
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, receipts, password resets)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the reading experience
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate SpeedRead:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — database hosting and user authentication. Supabase's Privacy Policy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery. Resend's Privacy Policy
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in authentication. Google's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and DDoS protection. Cloudflare's Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Aggregated, anonymized session data (reading speed trends, feature usage) may be retained indefinitely for analytics and product improvement.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your reading history and session data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@swiftread.app.

6. Cookies

SpeedRead uses only essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

SpeedRead is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us:

Email: support@swiftread.app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of SpeedRead after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
