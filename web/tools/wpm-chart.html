How to Move Up the Chart

Most people never train their reading speed after elementary school. That means the ceiling is much higher than where you currently sit. Here's how to move up:

  1. Benchmark first: Take the free speed test to know your starting point.
  2. Use RSVP: Rapid Serial Visual Presentation eliminates eye movement — the #1 bottleneck in reading speed. Learn how RSVP works →
  3. Train daily: 10 minutes/day is enough. WarpReader's 28-day course builds speed progressively.
  4. Monitor comprehension: Speed without retention is useless. Use WarpReader's AI quizzes to stay honest.
  5. Push intervals: Alternate between comfortable speed and 20% faster. Your brain adapts to the stretch.
